To my love, Ash

Poems

More to come every week

Loving you is like a summers day breeze,
You bring my heart a feeling of ease,

You're my future and you're my now,
You're a goddess and to you I'll bow,

Like the ocean, big and blue,
As long as I live, I'll love you.

As long as we're together, we watch the sky,
As long as we're together, the days go by,

As long as we're together, we have a friend,
As long as wer'e together, our love knows no end,

As long as were together, we'll never be alone,
And if we ever are apart, our love is still written in stone.

I love your lips when they're pressed against mine
There is nothing more I desire

Our hearts and souls both entwined
Nothing can tame the fire

As I sit back and watch you live your life
All I'll do is admire
